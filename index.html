The Hidden Pen Names

There are plenty of writers who write and remain undiscovered. These pen names hide their actual, realistic selves from their writer selves. This book will take you on a journey of emotions. It has all the thoughts & those emotions behind the writers who slipped their words to make them a part of this book. It deals with the agony, sadness, love, joy, and so many more emotions that writers have expressed through their words. There may be so many words and write-ups that you may find close enough to hide yourselves. It may feel just as if it is your life spilled on the papers. Our writers have written these inclusive pieces which we have presented before you to indulge.

Some Unsent Words of Life

We have always unsaid things for some people in our lives. Some unsaid love stories, some unsaid apologies and so much more. This book is all about such unsaid tales. It narrates the underlying, unexpressed, unsaid emotions of the writers who penned it down. It will be all about what the writers and the readers feel through the (unsaid) words. Sometimes, we feel a lot but express very less. This book expresses all that.
